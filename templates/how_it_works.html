The Technology Behind RankCV

Semantic Embeddings New

We use the all-MiniLM-L6-v2 transformer model from sentence-transformers to convert text into 384-dimensional vectors. This captures meaning — so "Python developer" and "Python programmer" score as highly similar.

Smart Chunking

Long CVs are split into ~150-word chunks before embedding. We score the best-matching chunk against your job description, ensuring a 2-page CV and a 10-page CV compete fairly.

Keyword Overlap

A secondary keyword-matching layer extracts important technical terms from the job description and checks their presence in each CV. This adds robustness for exact skill requirements.

Advanced PDF Extraction

We use pdfplumber for high-quality text extraction from modern PDFs, with PyPDF2 as a fallback. Handles multi-column layouts, tables, and formatted resumes.

Cosine Similarity

Measures the cosine of the angle between the job description embedding and each CV chunk embedding, determining how closely a candidate's experience matches the role.

Real-time Analysis

Get results in seconds, not hours. Process multiple CVs simultaneously with our optimized Python backend powered by PyTorch and scikit-learn.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RankCV

Currently, RankCV supports PDF files only. We use PyPDF2 to extract text content from PDF resumes. Ensure your CVs are text-based PDFs (not scanned images) for best results. Support for DOCX and other formats is planned for future releases.

RankCV uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) combined with cosine similarity. Here's how:

1. Vectorization: We convert both the job description and each CV into numerical vectors based on word importance.
2. Similarity Scoring: We calculate the cosine similarity between the job description vector and each CV vector.
3. Ranking: Candidates are sorted by similarity score (0-100%), with the closest matches appearing first.

This is a proven information retrieval technique used by search engines and recommendation systems.

Your data security is our priority. By default, CVs are processed in-memory and not stored permanently. You can enable "Save CVs" in your settings if you want to keep them for future reference. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your candidate data with third parties. For enterprise users, we offer additional compliance features including GDPR data deletion requests.
